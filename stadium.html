Emirates Stadium

The Emirates Stadium is a football stadium located in Holloway, London, England. It is the home stadium of Arsenal Football Club and has a seating capacity of 60,704. The stadium officially opened on 22 July 2006 and has since become an iconic landmark for the club and its supporters.
