Legendary Stats

THE 49 UNBEATEN RECORD:

  • Arsenal achieved a record-breaking run of 49 consecutive top-flight league games unbeaten from May 2003 to October 2004, surpassing Nottingham Forest's previous record of 42. However, their unbeaten streak came to an end in controversy with a harshly-awarded penalty leading to a 2-0 defeat against Manchester United at Old Trafford.

  • During this remarkable run, Arsenal also accomplished the feat of an unbeaten top-flight league season, a record only matched by Preston North End in 1888-89. It's worth noting that Preston North End played 22 league games that season, whereas Arsenal played 38.

  • In the 2003/4 Premiership season, Arsenal failed to score in only four matches, resulting in 0-0 draws against Birmingham, Newcastle, Fulham, and Manchester United. Prior to the Fulham match, Arsenal had scored in 46 consecutive home league matches, with their last failure to score at home dating back to a 0-3 loss against Middlesbrough on April 14, 2001.
